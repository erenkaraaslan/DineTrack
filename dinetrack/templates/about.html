We face the same dilemma many college students do - figuring out how to split restaurant bills and tips. Far too often we find ourselves wasting too much time doing quick maths in our heads as our waiters awkwardly stare at us after handing us the bill. So, we decided to create dinetrack, a tool to settle the issue once and for all.
